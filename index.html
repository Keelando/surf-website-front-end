About

Salish Sea Wave Conditions provides real-time marine data for Halibut Bank, English Bay, and the Strait of Georgia. Data is automatically collected from Environment Canada and NOAA buoys, processed locally, and displayed here for research and recreational awareness.

Features include hourly wave height, wind speed, storm surge forecasts, and temperature trends across the BC coast and Salish Sea region.

Please independently verify this data and do not base recreational activities around this information. This website is in no way affiliated with either environment Canada or NOAA.
