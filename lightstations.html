About Lightstation Reports

BC's staffed lightstations provide essential weather observations from remote coastal locations. Lightkeepers report wind speed and direction, sea state (wave height and conditions), swell intensity and direction, and visibility every 3 hours.

Report Schedule: 00:00, 03:00, 06:00, 09:00, 12:00, 15:00, 18:00, 21:00 UTC (subtract 8 hours for Pacific Standard Time, 7 hours for Pacific Daylight Time)
