About Wind Observations

Salish Sea Wind Conditions provides real-time wind data from coastal weather stations across the Strait of Georgia and surrounding areas. Data is automatically collected from Environment Canada and NOAA weather stations and buoys, updated hourly.

Wind measurements are typically 10-minute averages, with gusts showing the maximum instantaneous wind speed during that period. All wind directions indicate where the wind is coming from (meteorological convention).
