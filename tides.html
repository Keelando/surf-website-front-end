About Tide Data

Tide observations are updated every 30 minutes from DFO (Fisheries and Oceans Canada) water level stations. Predictions are astronomical tide forecasts based on harmonic constituents and are updated daily.

High and low tide times represent the predicted maximum and minimum water levels for the current day in Pacific Time.

Please independently verify this data and do not base recreational activities around this information.
